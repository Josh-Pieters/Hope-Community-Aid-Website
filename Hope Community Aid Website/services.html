Our Services

Food Parcel Distribution

We provide essential food parcels to families in need across Strandfontein. Our monthly distributions reach over 500 households, ensuring nutritional support with locally sourced goods.

Educational Workshops

Our workshops offer skills training in literacy, numeracy, and life skills to over 1,000 participants annually. Held weekly, these sessions empower individuals with tools for personal and professional growth.

Youth Mentorship Programs

Our youth mentorship programs provide personalized guidance and support to young people in the community, fostering leadership and resilience among the next generation.
